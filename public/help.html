How to use NBA Schedule

Spoiler-free NBA game schedule with rosters, player profiles, and saved players. All 30 teams, regular season through playoffs.

Getting started

The schedule

Upcoming and live games appear by default, grouped by date. Use the Conference pills to filter to Eastern or Western Conference games only. Use the Status pills to show only Upcoming, Live, Final, or Postponed games.

Spoiler control

Scores are hidden by default. Toggle Show scores in the Filters panel whenever you're ready to see them. Scores show for both live and completed games.

Search

Type any team name in the search bar to instantly filter the schedule to games featuring that team.

My Team & Favorites

★ Favorite teams

Open ★ Favorite teams in filters and tap the star next to any franchise. Favorited teams get a ★ marker in every game row so you can spot them instantly.

My Team

Once a team is favorited, tap ☆ My Team next to it to set it as yours. The entire app switches to that franchise's colors. Their games get a colored left border and their logo appears in the header.

★ Saved Players

Tap Roster on any game row to open that team's full roster, then tap any player to see their profile card with season stats. Hit ☆ Save player to add them to your list. Open ★ Players in filters to see all saved players — tap one to pull up their profile card, then tap ← Roster to jump to their team's full roster.

Saving games

★ Star a game

Every game row has a ★ button. Tap it to save that game. Open ★ Saved in filters to see only your saved games.

Mark as watched

Completed games show a ○ toggle. Tap it to mark the game watched. Enable Hide watched in filters to clear them from your schedule once you're done.

Teams & Rosters

Teams view

Switch to Teams using the slider at the top of the controls. All 30 franchises appear as a crest grid separated by conference. Tap any crest to open that team's full roster.

Roster button

Every team name in the schedule has a Roster pill next to it. Tap it to open the full roster — players grouped by position (PG / SG / SF / PF / C) with headshots and jersey numbers. The modal also shows the team's next upcoming games. Tap any player to open their profile card with season averages (PTS / REB / AST / STL / BLK / FG% / 3P% / FT%).

Time zones & display

Game times

Tip-off times default to your local time zone automatically. Open the Times shown in dropdown in Filters to lock to a specific zone — useful when traveling.

Mobile tips

On your phone
  • Tap Filters to expand the full control panel — it's collapsed by default on mobile.
  • The Teams slider takes you to the full franchise grid — great for browsing without an active game.
  • Add the app to your home screen (Share → Add to Home Screen on iOS) for a full-screen experience.
  • The schedule refreshes every 10 minutes — tap Refresh to force an update during live games.
  • All your settings (favorites, My Team, timezone) are saved locally and persist between visits.
